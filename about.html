About Agri Health

Our Purpose

Agri Health is designed to empower farmers and agricultural officers to monitor crop health efficiently. By combining technology with practical solutions, it provides real-time guidance to detect pests, protect crops, and optimize yield.

Features
  • Upload crop images for instant diagnosis
  • Receive actionable tips and preventive measures
  • Secure login and personalized access
  • Navigation to Home, Diagnosis, and About pages
  • Subtle animations and interactive design
Benefits
  • Time-saving pest detection
  • Reduce crop loss and increase yield
  • Technology-assisted farming for better efficiency
  • Educates farmers on crop management
Suggestions / Roadmap
  • Integrate real AI model for accurate pest detection
  • Add multi-crop support
  • Maintain history of past diagnoses
  • Mobile app version for on-field accessibility
